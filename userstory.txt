
Title: Log Waste Entry

User story:
As a household user, I want to manually log my waste items with details (name, category, weight, disposal method) to keep track of my waste generation.
Acceptance criteria:
•	Given that I am logged into the system,
•	When I enter waste details and submit,
•	Then the system stores the entry and displays it on my dashboard.


Title: View Waste History

User Story:

As a household user, I want to view my past waste logs to analyse my waste disposal trends.

Acceptance Criteria:

•	Given that I have logged waste in the past,
•	When I navigate to the waste history page,
•	Then I should see a list of my past waste entries with filter options.
