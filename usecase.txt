Title: Automated Waste Sorting for Waste Management Operators.
User Story:
 As a waste management operator, I want an AI tool that can help me automate waste sorting in the facility, so I can increase the efficiency and accuracy of the sorting process.
Acceptance Criteria: 
•	The AI system should be able to categorize large amounts of waste materials quickly and accurately.
•	It should integrate with the existing waste management system to assist with operational workflow.
